SR3V(Busse) - SR-3V is Busse Combat knives modification of Crucible CPM 3V steel. Composition doesn't seem to be altered, confirms to the same specs. Assuming Busse is using proprietary HT protocol as with the other SR series alloys, SR-101 and SR-77. Standard HRC for SR-3V is 61-63HRC, which I think benefits SR-3V more than 58-60HRC found elsewhere.
