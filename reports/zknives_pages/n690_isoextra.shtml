N690 ISOEXTRA(Bohler-Uddeholm) - Bohler-Uddeholm steel, recommended working hardness 58-60HRC. Similar to VG-10. Later, Aichi produced pretty much identical(with N690), ZA-18 steel, promoted to be better a performer than VG-10. ISOEXTRA signifies ESR technology used in making of this steel.

Manufacturing Technology - ESR

Country - Sweden(SE)
