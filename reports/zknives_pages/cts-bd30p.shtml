CTS-BD30P(Carpenter) - Carpenter's slightly modified version of the Crucible CPM S30V steel. Has trace amounts of Co and Ni. First appeared in summer 2011, and as of now(07/2011) is not even on the Carpenter's website. So far, Spyderco used it in Manix 2 sprint run.

Manufacturing Technology - PM

Country - United States(US)
