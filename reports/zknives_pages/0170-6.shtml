0170-6(Sharon Steel) - Cutlery steel, patented, developed specifically for cutlery by Sharon steel(now defunct). Similar with AISI 1095 steel, with added trace amounts of Vanadium, Chromium and Nickel. This was the original Carbon V steel, up until 1988 when Sharon Steel went Bankrupt. Apparently the patent is still in effect even today.
