CPM Rex T15(Crucible) - CPM® Rex® T15 is Crucible version of the AISI T15 tool steel. Made using their trademark CPM process. Crucible also makes HS(High Sulfur) version of this alloy, which is better machineable, but less desirable in knives, due to lowered wear resistance.
