PMC 27(Damasteel) - Razor steel by design. Very pure alloy, fine grained, good corrosion resistance. Takes very sharp edge thanks to very fine grain. Best performance with high polished, acute edge, it's a razor steel after all. This specific alloy is very close in composition with Sandvik 12C27 steel, however, it does benefit from PM technology, making it cleaner and further refining the grain. Unfortunately, PMC 27 is not available as a standalone steel, but only in combination with another Damasteel alloy RWL-34, the mix of those two making DS93x Damascus steel, which unlike usual pattern Damascus steels, is formed by mixing powders of those two steels(PMC27 and RWL-34) and then hot forming it.

Manufacturing Technology - PM
