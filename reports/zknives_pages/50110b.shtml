50110B(Sharon Steel) - Based on 1095 steel, with the addition of small amounts of V, Mo, Ni, Cr. Improved wear resistance and toughness compared to 1095. Ka-Bar and Bark river use this steel in their knives. Ka-Bar under 1095 Cro-Van name and Bark River as 50-110B. developed by now defunct Sharon Steel.
