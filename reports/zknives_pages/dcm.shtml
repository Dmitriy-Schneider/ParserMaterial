DCM(Novo) - Knife Steel FAQ - An excellent air-hardening tool steel, it is tougher than AISI D2 and AISI M2, with less wear resistance. As an air-hardening steel, don't expect it to be differentially tempered. Its good toughness makes it a frequent choice for combat knives.
