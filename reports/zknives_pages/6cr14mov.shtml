6Cr14MoV(Ahonest Changjiang) - Stainless alloy designed for use in knives, razors and other cutting tools. Slight improvement over GB 6Cr14 steel, in that 6Cr14MoV as its name suggests, adds trace amounts of Molybdenum and Vanadium. Both elements are positive additions for the knife blade alloys, although, in such small amounts it is not clear how much the actual improvement will be.
