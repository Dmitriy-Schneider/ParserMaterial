OHV6(Metal Ravne) - Low alloyed cold working tool steel. Designed for die casting tools, pressing tools, etc. Not too many carbide formers in it, so wear resistance isn't high, obviously it will outwear simple carbon steels like AISI 1095 steel and others from the 10xx series, but won't come close to high Vanadium or Molybdenum alloys like CPM 10V steel or any of the high speed tool steels. Still, decent thing with refined edge, depends what will you expect from it.

Manufacturing Technology - Ingot
