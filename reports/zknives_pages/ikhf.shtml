IKHF(GOST) - In Russian sources the 11KH4V2MF3S2 steel is listed as tool steel for cutting instruments with diameter up to 30mm. Composition is virually identical with Camillus Steel 1095 Cro-Van, which is quite popular in knives, you can read additional notes over there.
