Type 301(Chroma) - Proprietary alloy used by Chroma knives in their Porsche design knives. Exact composition is not published. However, Chroma representative did state Type 301 was based on AUS6M. Which could mean one of two things, the alloy composition was actually modified slightly, or it's just a rebrand. At any rate, Type 301 will be very close to AUS6M steel.
